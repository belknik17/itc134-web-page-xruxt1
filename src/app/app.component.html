Year Up offers a great oppurtunity for young adults who want to succeed in their career and life.

Internship

After completing their 6 months of learning, students are ready to join the corprorate world and intern at one of the companies partnered with Year Up.

Some of the 2019 Puget Sound partners are: Amazon, Inc., AT&T Inc., Boeing, Facebook, Inc., Google, Microsoft Corporation, Rosetta Stone.
